This page complies with **GDPR article 13** on international data transfers. For each model in the catalog we indicate where data is processed, whether the region is configurable, and under which legal framework the transfer is authorized. The table **is generated from the catalog**, so new providers appear automatically.

<Tip>
**🇪🇺 ZDR + European endpoint note**

When you enable [Zero Data Retention](/en/features/zdr) and select a provider with an EU endpoint (today: Google Vertex AI region `europe-west*` and OpenAI Azure Europe), **data never leaves the EU** — neither during processing nor after, since ZDR guarantees no provider-side persistence. This combination drives the international-transfer surface to zero.
</Tip>

## Legal frameworks in use

- **EU/EEA** — data does not leave the EU/EEA. Default framework (no international transfer basis required).
- **EU-US DPF** — *EU-US Data Privacy Framework*, adequacy decision adopted by the European Commission on July 10, 2023. Covers EU→US transfers to DPF-certified organizations.
- **SCC** — *Standard Contractual Clauses* (Decision (EU) 2021/914), module 2 controller-to-processor. Geek Hub signs SCC with each provider not covered by an adequacy decision.
